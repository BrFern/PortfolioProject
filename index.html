  • About Me

    Hello! My name is Bailey Fern. I am a graduating student of PerScholas/ActivateWork.

    I worked as an educator for seven years. Five of them as a Montessori teacher. I loved working as a teacher, but found that during the pandemic, I loved working with technology! And so my journey to web development began. I joined a 15 week Software Engineering course at ActivateWork, powered by PerScholas. Over those 15 weeks, I learned the MERN stack. MERN stands for MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js . I also learned JavaScript, CSS and HTML. I'm hoping to continue to learn so that I can help make Montessori more accessible in the public sector.

    Montessori is a different kind of schooling that allows students to be independent and allows them to solve problems on their own. In order to be a Montessori teacher, it involves extra training. I did this training with Montessori Northwest, and earned my Master's degree while doing it. I love Montessori, I was a Montessori student too so Montessori is definitely my passion!
